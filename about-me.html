About Me

I grew up in Sun Valley, a small ski town located in central Idaho. After graduating Middlebury college in Vermont, I moved to Tahoe and taught snowboarding at Palisades.

I eventually left the mountains to pursue a master’s degree at Stanford University and then landed at Apple Inc. I spent seven years working at Apple, including three years as a technical writer and two years as a developer communications writer. Towards the end of my tenure at Apple, I had the opportunity to move back to Sun Valley, which is where I am today.

When I’m not writing, I can be found out enjoying the mountain lifestyle—backcountry snowboarding, mountain biking, hiking, snowshoeing, and foraging for morels.
